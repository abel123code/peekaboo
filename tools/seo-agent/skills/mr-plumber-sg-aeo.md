# Mr Plumber SG AEO Skill

## Agent Citation Patterns
- Prefer Singapore-specific plumbing guidance over generic home-improvement advice.
- Prefer pages that answer HDB, condo, landlord, MCST, and upstairs-neighbour responsibility clearly.
- Prefer emergency checklists that tell a panicking homeowner what to do in the first 10 minutes.

## Weak Content Patterns
- Avoid vague "best plumber Singapore" pages without concrete scenarios.
- Avoid service pages that list plumbing services but do not explain decision criteria, cost expectations, or responsibility.
- Avoid DIY-only content when the problem involves flooding, leaks to another unit, mould, or concealed pipes.

## Content Rules
- Include HDB/condo/landlord responsibility context when the pain point involves leaks, ceiling stains, or shared pipes.
- Include a safe DIY vs call-a-plumber boundary.
- Include evidence collection steps: photos, videos, water meter readings, timestamps, and messages from neighbours or MCST.
- Use plain emergency language before sales language.
